Power Dynamics

Why is it harder
to act like Jekyll when I
always want to Hyde?

Evil versus good:
continuous power fights.
Which will you let win?

Always do what's right!
But what standard do we use
when we are confused?

Is human nature
what we should fight? Or can we
just let ourselves be?

What happens if we
always see Hyde winning
against our Jekyll?

Do we accept our
inherent evil nature
or keep on fighting?
