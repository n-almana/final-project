The Director's Stage

Acting
beyond myself and this facade.
Carrying my own weight
Don't look back now!
Even when times get rough
for now and forever
greater times ahead,
harder times ahead.
I know I have to play this role
just to survive, by
killing time or setting the scene,
living life and
making the most of whatever I have.
Never regretting
or wishing otherwise.
Pulling myself away from the director's space,
Qatar, the stage where my heart used to be
running away from the memories yet
smiling still because of them.
The same ones buried
under the trap door of shame,
virtues and encumbrances, one and the same
when it comes to my past plays. Like an algebraic variable:
X, whatever I want it to be, ever changing
yet always yearning for the lead role. I will not allow it. Undoubtedly
zealous with my intent. No applause necessary. No standing ovation.
