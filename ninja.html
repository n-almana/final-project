Ninja

"Of all forms of caution, caution in love is
perhaps the most fatal to true happiness."
- Bertrand Russell
Love is a ninja.
Quiet and quick.
No one sees it coming,
you just feel its nunchuks
leaving you disoriented.
Its stealth will turn your life upside down
and leave you struggling to get back up.
It doesn't come with a caution sign or a warning;
it silently crashes into every bit of your soul
and shatters you into a million little pieces
when it decides to leave.
Yes. I did say when.

Love shows up uninvited.
Love does not give a fuck.
It is rude and disruptive.
Love never follows social rules
And never does what you want it to.
It is never expected nor welcomed.
Love will cut you deeper than any shuriken.
Love will scar you in the most beautiful ways.
Love is worth it.

- I hope.
